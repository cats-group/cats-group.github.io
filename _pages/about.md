CATS (Computational Analysis of Text and Society) is an interdisciplinary research lab led by [Julia Mendelsohn](https://juliamendelsohn.github.io/) at the [University of Maryland College of Information](https://ischool.umd.edu/) and the [University of Maryland Institute for Advanced Computer Studies](https://www.umics.umd.edu/). We are also part of the broader [Computational Linguistics and Information Processing (CLIP) Lab](https://wiki.umiacs.umd.edu/clip/index.php/Main_Page).

We develop computational methods to study language, politics, and society. Our work combines natural language processing computational social science, and sociolinguistics to answer questions about the world around us.
